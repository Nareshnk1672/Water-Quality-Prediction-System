About the Project

The Water Quality Prediction System is a project that aims to predict water quality using machine learning algorithms. The system takes various input parameters such as pH, temperature, dissolved oxygen, and conductivity to predict the water quality.

Why is Water Quality Important?

Water quality is important for human health, ecosystem functioning, and economic activities. Poor water quality can lead to the spread of diseases, harm aquatic life, and negatively impact tourism and other industries that rely on clean water.
